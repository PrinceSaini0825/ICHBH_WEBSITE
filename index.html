OBJECTIVE OF THE CONFERENCE

VIT Bhopal University in association with AAB COLLEGE, Pristina Republic of Kosova is organizing The Second International Conference Humanities and Beyond the Humanities, from 21-22th November 2024.

The aim of The conference “Humanities and Beyond the Humanities: A Critical Approach to Language,Literature, Media & Management Studies” aims to advance critical perspectives and unite scholars, researchers, and practitioners in exploring the complexities of human culture, language, literature, media, and cultural practices.It fosters interdisciplinary dialogue and collaboration among participants from diverse fields.

Some of the key highlights: -

  • Encourages critical engagement with language, literature, media, and cultural practices
  • Seeks to unveil hidden biases and inequities in these fields
  • Provides a platform for interdisciplinary dialogue and collaboration
  • Promotes pedagogical innovation in teaching language, literature, media, and cultural studies
  • Facilitates the establishment of collaborative research networks and academic partnerships

    About VIT Bhopal University

The University is witnessing tremendous growth under the guidance of Dr. Senthil Kumar Arumugam, Vice Chancellor (I/c). They share the mission to make VIT a global center of education. Starting from the 2017 academic year, the new Ultra-modern university campus, sprawling over 300 acres, is in the town of Kothri-Kalan, District Sehore, surrounded by green, lush fields and a serene environment.
