Conference Themes

Academics, educators, researchers, and practitioners from various fields are invited to submit their original research work related to the topics mentioned here.

The conference theme is not limited to the above-mentioned topics. Any research work related to interdisciplinary approaches in humanities is also welcomed.

All research papers should present novel and/or innovative viewpoints within the general scope of the conference's themes. Every submission will undergo a peer review process.

*Only presented papers at the conference with high academic rigor and interdisciplinary focus will proceed for formal review for publication.
